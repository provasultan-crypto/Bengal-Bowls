The Story

From Bogra, Bangladesh to Potsdam, NY and with many stops in between, spreading Bangladeshi culture and cuisine is an important way I stay connected to my roots, and build community wherever I go. Over the past several decades I have built many friendships over fresh, delicious, meals.

In 2023 I was inspired by my husband’s health journey to intentionally focus on heart healthy cuisine that looks good, tastes good, and feels good.

From bright veggies to healing spices, every dish is made to nourish, comfort, and bring a little joy to your table.

No guilt, just delicious!
